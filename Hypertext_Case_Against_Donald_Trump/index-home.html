The Case Against Donald Trump

A Hypertext Narrative in Fall 2022

Donald Trump looms large in the Republican party and in the United States. He, along with many members of his party, is working to destabilize our Democratic Republic.

It is rumored that he will run again for the office of the presidency in 2024.

This project will outline some of the reasons why he should never be in the White House again.
